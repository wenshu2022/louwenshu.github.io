Deep Learning Approaches to Climate Change Prediction

Lead Researcher: Dr. Jane Smith

Date: January 2020 - December 2022

Abstract

This research focuses on the development and application of advanced deep learning models to predict climate change impacts more accurately over the next century. The study leverages large datasets of historical weather patterns and carbon emission records to train predictive models.
